## Design objective
Make the site feel premium, strategic, and slightly sharp, not template-safe or generic.

## Design traits
- editorial
- dark but readable
- high-contrast
- serious
- slightly playful in motion, not childish
- confident without overdecoration

## Visual message
The design should feel like:
- systems
- discipline
- product gravity
- technical sharpness

## Avoid
- overusing giant generic slogans
- default SaaS blandness
- purple-gradient AI clichés
- decorative geometry with no content value

## Section emphasis
- flagship projects should feel weightier than support cards
- directed products should feel active and alive, but not unfinished
- proof strip should look like a trust layer, not vanity metrics

## Typography direction
- bold, memorable display treatment for hero
- cleaner editorial body copy
- crisp metadata styling

## Motion direction
- reveal and stagger okay
- no trapped carousels
- no interaction blockers
