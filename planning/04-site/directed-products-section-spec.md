## Purpose
Publicly represent serious products that are actively being built under Cheker Hassan’s direction and supervision.

## Naming
Preferred section labels:
- Directed Products
- Products In Development

Recommended default:
- Directed Products

## Why this section exists
- to show active ecosystem building
- to represent supervised product work honestly
- to avoid forcing these products into either full flagship or hidden status

## Card content
Each item should show:
- title
- one-line framing
- who it is for
- status
- role framing: builder + director
- CTA

## Disclosure rule
The section should clearly communicate:
- active development
- product direction and supervision
- serious architecture and team structure

It should not communicate:
- weak “coming soon” filler
- fake launch maturity

## CTA style
- Case study
- Request walkthrough
- Architecture view

## Homepage role
- sits after flagships
- acts as the bridge between proven public work and the wider product ecosystem
