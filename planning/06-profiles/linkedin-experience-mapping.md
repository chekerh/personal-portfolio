## Purpose
Map projects into experience-style entries without making the profile look like a random list of personal repos.

## Recommended experience buckets
### Product Engineer / Builder + Director
- umbrella entry for supervised products, systems, and multi-project direction

Use this entry to group:
- Local-Arcads
- FlashForge
- Context Assistant
- WayFinder
- SignLik
- CogniCare

### Selected Product Systems
- CheckAuto TN
- DevisPilot
- Sahara
- TrustKit

## Role phrasing
Use:
- shaped product direction
- structured delivery
- supervised execution
- contributed technically
- moved systems toward launch clarity

Avoid:
- pretending to be only a manager
- pretending to be sole implementer on all team-built systems

## Date strategy
- use honest overlapping ranges where projects were concurrent
- let the umbrella entry absorb multi-product supervision

## Featured section defaults
- `CheckAuto TN`
- `DevisPilot`
- `Sahara`
- one selected directed product only if framed clearly as in development

## Consistency rule
- LinkedIn Experience should sound slightly more conservative than the portfolio homepage
- LinkedIn should not introduce project names that do not already exist in the canonical inventory
