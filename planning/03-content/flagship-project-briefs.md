## CheckAuto TN
- Hook: A trust-first verification platform built to reduce risk in used-car buying workflows.
- Problem: Vehicle buyers and operators need clearer, more reliable verification processes than informal trust signals.
- System framing: multi-surface trust platform with auth, RBAC, reports, admin workflows, billing foundations, and async processing.
- Stack: Next.js, React, NestJS, Prisma, PostgreSQL, Redis, BullMQ.
- Role framing: builder + director of a serious product system with strong operations shape.
- Why it matters: demonstrates real product logic, admin depth, and production-oriented system design.
- Detail-page priority: highest.

## DevisPilot
- Hook: A SaaS system for service businesses that turns lead capture into structured sales follow-up.
- Problem: small operators need better lead handling, qualification, and commercial follow-through.
- System framing: multi-tenant SaaS with onboarding, CRM-lite logic, demo-ready commercial framing, and local-market fit.
- Stack: Next.js, Prisma, PostgreSQL, Clerk.
- Role framing: builder + director shaping a monetizable SMB SaaS product.
- Why it matters: clean business story, local relevance, strong product-market framing.
- Detail-page priority: highest.

## Sahara
- Hook: A local AI IDE built for developers who want serious tooling, not just another wrapper.
- Problem: developers need tighter local-first agent workflows, coding assistance, and control over their tooling stack.
- System framing: Electron-based desktop environment with local AI, editor tooling, and workflow depth.
- Stack: Electron, React, TypeScript, Monaco, local AI/service architecture.
- Role framing: builder + director with strong hands-on technical depth.
- Why it matters: raises the technical ceiling of the portfolio.
- Detail-page priority: highest.

## TrustKit
- Hook: A monetizable testimonial SaaS with a clear buyer story and simple path to value.
- Problem: businesses need structured testimonial collection and usable social proof surfaces.
- System framing: forms, review flow, public wall, widget embedding, and billing.
- Stack: Flask, billing/web workflows, embeddable UI.
- Role framing: product-minded builder with clear SaaS taste.
- Why it matters: cleaner commercial story than many student-style apps.
- Detail-page priority: medium-high.

## Local-Arcads
- Hook: A local-first cross-hardware ad-generation platform built like future subscription software, not a throwaway AI demo.
- Problem: creators and operators need local control, hardware-aware runtime support, and production-shaped ad generation workflows.
- System framing: web studio, runtime doctoring, queueing, creative pipeline, voice/visual/editor stages, monetization and ops direction.
- Stack: Python, config-driven pipeline, local runtimes, web surface, workflow orchestration.
- Role framing: builder + director shaping the product system, runtime logic, and development structure.
- Why it matters: shows ecosystem thinking, runtime depth, and future-product seriousness.
- Detail-page priority: high, with in-development framing.
