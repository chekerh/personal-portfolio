## Role-target variants
### General software engineering
- emphasize flagship builds
- include 1 builder + director bullet max
- keep directed-product framing selective and disciplined

### Product / delivery / technical coordination
- emphasize builder + director bullets
- include 1 to 2 directed-product bullets
- use phrasing around roadmaps, coordination, and launch readiness

### Greece-focused applications
- emphasize communication range, seriousness, and product ownership
- favor cleaner flagship bullets over experimental wording
- keep the tone premium but conservative

## Exclusion rules
- do not stack too many similar SaaS bullets
- do not overload the CV with in-development products
- do not use any bullet that sounds like a founder-post slogan instead of a concrete responsibility

## Variant emphasis
- General engineering: emphasize systems built and stacks used
- Product/delivery-heavy: emphasize structure, supervision, and launch shaping
- Greece-focused: emphasize breadth, seriousness, communication, and readiness
